My First Story was formed in the summer of 2011 in Shibuya, Tokyo.
Since their debut in April 2012 with their 1st Album「MY FIRST STORY」, the band has grown to become a hot topic, and they have continued to improve their skills through performing at festivals nationwide as well as co-starring with overseas artists.

2016
They released their 4th Album「ANTITHESE」which placed 4th on the Oricon Weekly rankings, going on to perform at 47 prefectures in Japan with the album. The final performance took place at the Nippon Budokan and sold out, with an audience of 12,000 people.

2017
They held their「MMA」tour, with 18,000 people being present for the final performance at Makuhari Messe International Exhibition Hall in Chiba.

2018
The「S・S・S」Tour brought the band nationwide and they held their first hall tours in Osaka, Fukuoka, Sendai and Nagoya. Ending the performance with their first 2-day live at Yokohama Arena.

2019
During the「MY FIRST STORY TOUR 2019」, the band held arena tours at Kobe World Memorial Hall and Saitama Super Arena in addition to the live house and hall versions.

2020
「MY FIRST STORY TOUR 2020」and 「MY FIRST STORY "V" TOUR」are held, consisting of 12 performances over 6 days at the Pacifico Yokohama National Hall in Kanagawa.

2021
In January, they held a short tour in Tokyo, Nagoya and Osaka. In February, they held their first performance at the Nippon Budokan in five years as part of the 「TOUR FINAL」.
The band's first Tokyo hall performance took place in Shibuya, where the band was formed, as part of the 「TOUR FINAL」.
Their 6th album,「V」, released in August and placed 2nd on the Oricon Weekly Ranking, their highest ranking ever achieved.

2022
Commemorating the 10th anniversary of the band, they released the album「X」containing their best hits based on fan requests.
「MY FIRST STORY 2022」tour held at live houses and halls with 22 performances nationwide.

2023
The band is aiming for 12 consecutive months of music releases.
Their first 47-prefecture tour in Japan after 7 years, 「MY FIRST STORY -THE TWO-」will be held.
